Gauging Interest in possible July 17-21, 2017 Towson Bible Conference

A few E Bible Fellowship (EBF) listeners have brought up the idea of having a Bible conference (similar to the ones FR use to hold) this summer. One of the possible locations for this conference is Towson University in Maryland. Towson was the site of FR’s last Bible conference on the east coast several years ago.

Before we make a decision on holding this conference EBF first needs to know the number of people that are seriously interested in attending. If we were to book the facility we would need to let Towson know how many people would be attending and then be responsible for paying for that number of rooms. Here’s some information about the time and cost for the event:

Time: July 17th thru July 21th (Monday thru Friday). Arrival on Monday and leave on Friday.
Cost: About 400 per person (including meals/tax). Not sure at this point the cost for a family or a family with children.

Again, please only indicate you are interested in attending if you seriously think you can afford it and plan on going. If you’re are seriously interested in attending and would like more information as it becomes available, please send an email to: ebiblefellowship@juno.com

Thank you and may the Lord’s perfect will be done.
