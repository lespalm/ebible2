The only remaining question is: how long will it be until God destroys the world? E Bible Fellowship is continuing to study all that the Bible has to say regarding this question of the timing for the end of the world.

At this time, there is evidence for 2033 AD. The evidence is NOT conclusive. E Bible is not saying this will or will not be the date. We are only presenting the available evidence for all to check out. It may be that another date comes forth, perhaps in two years or five. We are not ruling out any possibilities at this point.
