Time Paths Modified with Ending of 45/46

We’ve known for some time that God modifies time paths by 2300 days (6 years) or by 23 years. The fullness of a number is reached and then 6 years or 23 years are added on before the major event takes place. The flood occurring 6023 years from creation is one example of this. Also, Jesus’ birth occurring 11,006 years from creation is another.

I’ve recently noticed that there appears to be another modifying number. The number 45 or 46 also is used by God to modify a number once its reached its fullness. Here are some examples of this:

2945 years from creation---End of Mahaleel’s period and year Jared was born (11,013 - 8068 BC = 2945 years)

8846 years from creation---Birth of Abram to Terah. Terah 130 years old (11,013 - 2167 BC = 8846 years)

8945 years from creatoin---Circumcision of Abraham at age 99 (11,013 - 2068 BC = 8945 years)

8946 years from creation---Birth of Isaac. Abraham age 100 (11,013 - 2067 BC = 8946 years)

10046 years from creation---Foundation of temple laid in Solomon’s 4th year (11,013 - 967 BC = 10046 years)

11,045/46 years from creation---Christ goes to the cross (11,013 + 33 AD = 11,046 calendar years/11,045 actual years)

13,045/46 years from creation---Possible end of world as Christ destroys it (11,013 BC + 2033 AD = 13,046 calendar years/13,045 actual years)

The existence of 7 time paths ending in 45/46, especially since it includes the major time path from creation to the cross, shows that this is modifying number is indeed something to be considered when looking at the available Biblical evidence.
